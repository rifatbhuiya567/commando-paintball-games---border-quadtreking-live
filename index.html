about us

Commando Paintball and Border Quad Trekking is situated in the picturesque surroundings of the Shropshire, Mid Wales Borders (near SHREWSBURY), . We are a family run outdoor pursuits centre based at the beautiful Bulthy Hill Farm.

All staff members and facilitators are seasoned professionals, and qualified instructors in a plethora of activities, such as Quad Biking,Paintballing, Archery, Laser Sports, Corporate team building activities,And our off site mobile activities.

Accommodation can be arranged to fit your package.

We continue to follow all government guidelines for Covid 19.

Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions regarding our covid safety measures.

We can cater for

rage buggies

Rage buggies as featured on BBC Two's Top Gear and voted the best off road vehicle are the best vehicles on the market to get the adrenalin pumping.

quad biking

With the help of our experienced and patient instructors quad Biking is a challenging and exciting day for all Taking in the surrounding countryside, through undiscovered paths and hills .

paintballing

Paintballing is a sport that combines teamwork, strategy and skill into an exciting and action packed day During your day you will be set an exciting variety of objectives and missions, all designed to test your abilities to the full in a fun atmosphere.

corporate entertainment

Commando Paintball will make your launch, incentive, prize or general team building day fun, exciting and challenging in a superb relaxing enviroment. Transport and food available for any numbers.We can cater the day for all your needs.

family fun day

Bulthy Farm is a fantastic place for adults and children alike with the combination of children's quad bikes and the lambing season. It makes an interesting and exciting day. Bring your picnic or bike up to the top of Bulthy Hill. Let the family explore the site before they come by visiting the Gamezone.

stag hen parties

we can organise your party from start to finish, with affordable clean accomodation, situated close to Shrewsbury. Not only can your party have a challenging outdoor pursuits weekend you can also have boozy nights at the local clubs.

laser clays

Lasersport can be enjoyed by people of all ages. It can be played just for fun, or as a serious competition with people participating as individuals or as part of a team. As with any sport, there are different levels and classes of Lasersport enabling it to be played by everyone. We can offer Laser Clays at your own venue if required, and our Party Tent and Barbeque hire could make your day even more special.

archery

Are you more robin hood or robin Williams? archery is a skilful sport requiring balance, poise, accuracy, vision, timing, action - they all combine in a test of nerve.
